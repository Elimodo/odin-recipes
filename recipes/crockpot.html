Crockpot Italian Chicken

All the ingredients needed to prepare the meal are crockpot Italian chicken, Italian dressing, and Parmesan cheese

Ingredients

  1. One bottle Italian dressing, for example, Olive GardenSM Signature Italian Dressing
  2. Quarter cup grated Parmesan cheese
  3. Atleast four boneless skinless breasts

Directions

  1. Combine the Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese in a bowl
  2. Place the chicken in a slow cooker, and pour the dressing and parmesan cheese mixture over the chicken.
  3. Cover and cook on low heat until the chicken is tender and no longer pink in the center and juices run clear [about 8 hours]
